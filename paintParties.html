We Offer Private Paint Parties

Kids Parties

We provide children's paint parties where kids can express their creativity. Including birthdays or social events, march break activities, workshops, and more.

  • We can come to your home / venue or we can host your party in our studio
  • We are a mobile business and can bring tables and chairs with us when we travel to your location
  • We provide all of the tools including paints, paint brushes, canvasses, aprons, easels, etc.
  • The duration is a total of 3 hours, which includes 1 hour for setup and cleanup and 2 hours of painting
  • Our fees are per person, for children's party rates please call or e-mail us and we provide you with our promotional rate of the day
