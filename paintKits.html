First, choose your painting design from the different patterns. (see patterns below)
Our paint kits come with:

  • Step-by-Step painting instructions for your chosen design
  • 2 flat canvases, 7" X 9"
  • Paints appropriate for your design
  • Set of 5 paint brushes of different sizes
  • Water cup for the brushes
  • Plastic table protector
  • 2 Styrofoam plates for mixing paint
  • Paper napkins

Our Kids paint kits come with:

  • Pre-drawn canvases, ready to be coloured in
  • Paints (assorted colours)
  • Set of 5 paint brushes of different sizes
  • Water cup for the brushes
  • Plastic table protector
  • Styrofoam plates for mixing paint
  • Wooded or clay sculpture, ready to be painted
  • Age appropriate stickers
  • Paper napkins
