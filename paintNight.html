Paint Night Events

We offer public and private paint night events that take place at Wild Wing restaurant in Thornhill ON.
Come join us for a night of fun where you can express your inner artist with the help of our instructors, while having a glass of wine or dinner. A great date night idea or plan it for a girl's night out!
